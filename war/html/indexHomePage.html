S-LOR (Sensor-based Linked Open Rules) is a rule-based reasoning engine and an approach for sharing and reusing interoperable rules to deduce meaningful knowledge from sensor measurements.

Check all NEWS regarding our research and this web site

We have demos illustrating the research:
  • Demo V1: Get all rules specific to one sensor
  • Demo V2: Choose a domain, select a sensor specific to the domain to retrieve all rules
